About Me

I completed my Ph.D. in Physics from UT Austin in 2025, where I was advised by Feliciano Giustino. I earned an M.S. in Computational Science, Engineering, and Mathematics from the Oden Institute in 2024. Earlier, I studied at Wuhan University in China, and worked at HKUST under the supervision of Xi Dai.

My research spans many-body physics and first-principles methodologies for real systems, and lies at the intersection of quantum materials, quantum geometry, and quantum optics, aiming to design and control materials functionalities for information and energy applications.
